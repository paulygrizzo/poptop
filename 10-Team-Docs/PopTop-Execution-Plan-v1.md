## Executive Summary

This document outlines the systematic approach to take PopTop from **concept to market** before the 2025 College Football season. The plan is organized into **6 phases** with clear milestones, owners, and dependencies.

**Critical Path:** Design Freeze → Prototype Validation → Licensing Approval → Production → Launch

## Critical Dependencies & Risks

### Dependencies
```
Design Freeze (Week 8) → Manufacturing RFQ (Week 8)
Tooling Complete (Week 16) → T1 Samples (Week 17)
CLC Approval (Week 18) → Licensed Production (Week 19)
T1 Approval (Week 17) → Production Run (Week 18)
Inventory Receipt (Week 20) → Launch (Week 22)
```

### Risk Register
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Tooling delays | Medium | High | Early kick-off, buffer time |
| CLC approval delays | Medium | High | Early application, multiple schools |
| Manufacturing quality issues | Low | High | T1 validation, QC process |
| Capital constraints | Medium | Medium | Phased approach, pre-orders |
| Design iteration needs | Medium | Medium | Rapid prototyping capability |
